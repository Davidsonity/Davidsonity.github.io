About Me

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, which has provided me with a strong foundation in mathematics and a deep understanding of machine learning concepts. During my time as a Data Analyst at Cleanpick Green Nigeria Limited, I worked extensively with data, extracting valuable insights to support decision-making processes. I have pursued further education through programs such as the Udacity Data Analytics Nanodegree, IBM Data Science Professional Certificate, IBM Machine Learning Professional Certificate, and the AWS AI & ML scholarship program.

Proficiency in Python programming is one of my key strengths, and I have practical experience with popular frameworks like Pandas, Seaborn, Plotly, Scikit-learn, PyTorch, and TensorFlow. Additionally, I have expertise in SQL and Google Cloud ML. Currently, I am actively engaged as a Freelance Data Scientist at Upwork Inc., where I have successfully completed multiple projects, all showcased on my portfolio website.

Throughout my career as a Machine Learning Engineer and Data Scientist, I have been involved in the design, development, and implementation of various machine learning models to tackle diverse problems. I am well-versed in tasks such as forecasting, model evaluation, providing recommendations, uncovering patterns, improving data quality, and effectively communicating insights to stakeholders. My skill set covers a wide range, including deep learning, recommender systems, data analysis, data visualization, and web scraping.

To validate my expertise in machine learning, I have obtained certifications such as the Google Cloud Machine Learning Professional, IBM Machine Learning Professional, and IBM Data Science Professional. These certifications acknowledge my proficiency in a variety of machine learning techniques, including unsupervised learning, supervised learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, time series analysis, and survival analysis.

Throughout my journey, I have completed numerous impactful projects, including Gender By Voice (Clustering), Stranger Things 4 (Sentiment Analysis), Restaurant Ratings Prediction, House Prices Prediction, Car Prices Prediction, Netflix Recommender System, Heart Failure Classifier, Nature Scene Classifier, and Restaurants Recommender System and much more.
