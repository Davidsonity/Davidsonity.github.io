About Author

Emuejevoke Eshemitan is a data enthusiast and devoted python developer. He is currently working as a freelancer in the data space, where he works with various teams and people from different regions around the world. He is Proficient in programming with Python and using frameworks like Pandas, Seaborn, Plotly, Scikit learn, OpenCV, TensorFlow, amongst others. He is also competent with SQL. As a Data Scientist, he develops strategies for analyzing and visualizing data, building models with data using python programming language. As a Machine Learning Engineer, he focuses on researching, building and running artificial intelligence systems to automate predictive models.

As an Engineering student at University of Lagos, He has acquired math skills involving probability, statistics, linear algebra and calculus. This made it easy for him to understand the concepts of machine learning. With the wide range of resources available on the internet, He started his journey towards becoming a Data Scientist and landed his first data related job as a Freelance Data Analyst for Cleanpick Green Nigeria Limited, a startup company. To gain more coding experience, He has completed the Udacity’s Data Analytics Nanodegree program, Coursera’s IBM Machine Learning Professional Certificate and IBM Data Science Professional Certificate. He is part of the 2000 people selected for the 2022 AWS AI & ML scholarship program. He has also completed the Udacity’s AI Programming with Python Nanodegree program. These programs have drastically helped in enhancing my skills.

He is currently preparing for the DP-100 examination to get Microsoft certified as an Azure data scientist associate.
